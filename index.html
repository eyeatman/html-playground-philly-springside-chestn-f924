Coding in Popular Culture (And how it cuases me grief)

When people talk using the many acronyms and terms of programming languages, many of them have no idea what they are doing. Assumptions on what something stands for, how something is pronounced, and other misconceptions can be a breeding ground of confusion for anyone, even experienced programmers. Needless to say, it is very easy to completely baffle folks like me with all these errors

An example of confusion due to mispronunciation

In this case, the resulting argument got me thinking there were actually two terms. I in my programming ignorance have been led to several similar misconceptions, all with embarrasing results.
